# RSS Podcast Feed

Subscribe to your personalized medical research podcast feed to listen on any podcast app.

## Feed URL
```
https://yourdomain.com/api/papers/podcast.rss
```

Replace `yourdomain.com` with your actual domain.

## Supported Podcast Apps

This RSS feed works with all standard podcast applications:

- **Apple Podcasts** (iOS/Mac)
- **Pocket Casts** (iOS/Android)
- **Overcast** (iOS)
- **Castro** (iOS)
- **Spotify** (via RSS import)
- **Google Podcasts** (Android)

## How to Subscribe

### Apple Podcasts (iOS)
1. Open the Podcasts app
2. Go to Library → Shows
3. Tap the ⋯ menu in the top right
4. Select "Add a Show by URL"
5. Paste your RSS feed URL

### Pocket Casts
1. Open Pocket Casts
2. Tap the Discover tab
3. Search icon → "Enter RSS URL"
4. Paste your RSS feed URL

### Overcast
1. Open Overcast
2. Tap the + button
3. Select "Add URL"
4. Paste your RSS feed URL

## What's in the Feed?

The RSS feed includes all your active topics (created within the last 24 hours):

- **Episode Title** - Your topic name + paper count
- **Description** - Brief summary of the collection
- **Audio** - Automatically generated narration
- **Publication Date** - When you created the topic

## Important Notes

- Feed updates in real-time as you create new topics
- Episodes disappear after 24 hours (when topics expire)
- The feed is personal to your session - others can't access your topics
- Audio is generated on first playback and then cached
